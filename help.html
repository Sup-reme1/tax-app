Help & Support

What happens if i miss a new Tax deadline? ›
Missing a tax deadline may result in penalties or interest charged by your local tax authority. The amount depends on how late the filing or payment is. If you miss a deadline, submit your tax information as soon as possible to reduce additional charges.
How do I update my personal information? ›
You can update your personal information at any time from your account setting. Go to profile, make the necessary changes, and save to keep your information up to date.
How do I change my Password? ›
To change your password, open Account settings, select security, and choose Change Password. Enter your current password and confirm your new one. if you forgot your password, use the Forgot Password option on the sign-in screen.
Can I use TaxPal without tax knowledge? ›
Yes. TaxPal is designed for everyone, including users with no tax experience. The app guides you step by step, explains tax terms clearly, and handles calculations automatically.
